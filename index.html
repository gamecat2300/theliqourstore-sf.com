Event Catering Norfolk

the-liqour-store is a private catering company based in central Norfolk, our aim is to create the perfect catering event, no matter how big or small. We are dedicated to providing delicious food, perfect event design and superb service.

Whether you are organising the biggest day of your life or - an intimate meal, the secret of success lies in developing a really good relationship with your caterer. We will work closely with you to ensure your - special event is professionally managed, beautifully presented and completely stress free, each event personalised to your precise requirements, we plan everything down to the smallest detail. All menus are bespoke to your budget and requirements. We look forward to working closely with our clients, discussing all options and creating your unique occasion.

We have built a dedicated development kitchen in central Norfolk to help us create & develop our dishes for every client. We also have designed a tasting area in the kitchen for clients to have tastings of their menus prior to their events.

Although we are based in central Norfolk, we are more than happy to cater for clients across the whole of East Anglia and have also worked at many locations throughout the UK and Europe.

Like all good caterers we are passionate about our ingredients. We work with great suppliers and likeminded Norfolk & UK producers whose focus is on quality and provenance.

Services

Private Dining

We can create a bespoke service for any private catering event, whether it is an intimate dinner for 2 or an event for 250 guests. We can create tailor-made packages to suit your requirements.

We understand that sometimes the best venue is your own home. We come, cook, serve and clean, taking care of everything you need to create the perfect event. All you need to do is invite your guests, relax and enjoy excellent food, fine wine in familiar surroundings.

Event & Corporate Catering

We cater for events of any size and occasion. Weddings, birthdays, christenings, shoot dinners or corporate events - if you are holding an event, whatever size or scale the-liqour-store are able to help with your catering needs. We can host at any location however far it may be.

Our event catering is tailored to suit the venue and exact requirements of the individual client.

Personal Chef

Whether you are looking for someone to take care of all your catering requirements for a long weekend, or to provide all day catering: breakfast, lunch and dinner , or perhaps you require a chef to cook for you wherever you are travelling. the-liqour-store can help you.

Event Management

A complete event design service, we will be with you from first ideas to completion. We can provide help with the design and planning, oversee your event, provide the catering and staff. the-liqour-store has built up great relationships with various suppliers, to supply and create the whole package. Whether it is from floral arrangements to marquee hire, we are happy to advise; creating an inspired, bespoke and totally unique event for every occasion.

About Me

‘the-liqour-store’ has been established by me, Jake Weatherill. I have spent my time working in some of the best restaurants in the UK and travelling the world building up my knowledge in exquisite food, flawless service and event planning. For many years I have worked with a number of exclusive private clients, and as demand has grown and reputation built, I have created ‘the-liqour-store’.

Having grown up in Norfolk, I have gradually a built a great relationship with my selected suppliers, and with them I am able to offer you some of the best quality produce in the country.

‘the-liqour-store’ is unique in the private catering market, offering luxury, restaurant quality food in the comfort of your own home or at private events.
